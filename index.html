O descendant of Bharata! At that time Śrī Kṛṣṇa, smiling, in the middle of both armies, spoke the following words to the grieving one (Arjuna).

श्रीभगवानुवाच ।
त्वम् अशोच्यानन्वशोचः प्रज्ञावादांश्च भाषसे ।
गतासूनगतासूंश्च पण्डिताः नानुशोचन्ति ।

Śrī Kṛṣṇa sagte,
du trauerst über die nicht betrübten Dinge und sprichst Worte der Weisheit.
Die Gelehrten trauern weder über die Lebenden noch über die Toten.

Śrī Kṛṣṇa said,
while speaking words of wisdom, you are grieving over what is not worthy of grief.
Those who are wise lament neither for the living nor the dead.
